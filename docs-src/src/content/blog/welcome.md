This is where I'll share my journey through art.

I started Palette as a way to track the paintings I was discovering across museums. What began as a simple tool became something more personal — a way to document how art was changing the way I see the world.

This journal is an extension of that. A place to write about the pieces that stop me in my tracks, the museums that surprise me, and the slow process of building a relationship with art when you didn't grow up with it.

More soon.
